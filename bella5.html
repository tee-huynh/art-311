Avoid the house.


You decide not to go inside the house. It’s too creepy and who knows what is inside. It’s better to be safe than sorry, you tell yourself.

You keep walking until you are at the edge of the town. You weren’t able to find anything.

Maybe it was best to just leave town right away.

All of a sudden, you hear loud footsteps coming your way. “Get out!”

Before you have time to react, the frantic man pushes you over the ledge of the cliff and you stumble and lose your footing.

Everything goes black.
