Set up Camp


You don't know why...but you decide to stay here in this cave.

You eventually start hearing that the sick have been turning into some sort of cannibal monsters, and that just about every child has passed away from the disease.

Maybe you'll be more use to people by setting up camp here, and helping out bypassers.

You see a vision. A child, attempting to get down this cave's hill, soon after, a landslide. You're not sure what this means...are you getting sick as well? No...you're not coughing.

You decide to create a secret entrance to the cave and mark it , maybe if this vision is a prophecy, the child's curiosity will lead him or her safely down the hill.
